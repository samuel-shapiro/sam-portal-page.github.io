Story Ending One

All of the Marshmallows in the bag weeped as their father was consumed by Jimmy. They began plotting their escape. The mother hurdled the children togther and tore through the bag. They ran through the woods. Meanwhile, the campers starved to death. The Marshmallows hid in a hole at the bottom of the tree, which was in a terrific school district. The children grew up well-fed and pampered. The wolves always left them alone.






































































Story Ending Two

The Marshmallow emerged from the flames, not broken, but invigoured. He grew 100x his size, tore off his charred outer skin, and then sat on the campers. The campers feebly tried to chew through the Marshmallow, but failed and suffocated in his fluffy body.
